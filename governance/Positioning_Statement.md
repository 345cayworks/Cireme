# CIREME Positioning Statement — v1 (Published)

> Phase 1 governance deliverable. Derived from and bound by the **locked Phase 0
> decision: Complement + Niche, no compensation lock-in**. This statement is
> authoritative; product, legal, and engineering decisions must remain
> consistent with it.

## What CIREME is

CIREME is an **open real-estate listing marketplace for the Cayman Islands**,
serving **private sellers (FSBO)** and **independent brokers/agents** who are
not required to belong to any incumbent association. It is a technology and
marketing platform with MLS-grade data discipline.

## What CIREME is not

CIREME is **not** a brokerage, a regulator, a self-regulatory body, an escrow
agent, a valuer, or a party to any transaction. It does not adjudicate
professional conduct, licensing, or client disputes.

## The defining promise: no compensation lock-in

CIREME does not require, set, mediate, publish, or enforce any buyer-broker
cooperation fee or commission split as a condition of listing, and provides
**no structured field** for advertising cooperation compensation. This is a
core architectural and contractual commitment, enforced in the data model (the
schema contains no compensation/cooperation fields) and in the Membership
Agreement §3.

## Strategic stance: Complement + Niche

CIREME complements rather than confronts the incumbent market by serving the
underserved FSBO and independent-broker segment. It does not depend on, or seek
interoperability obligations with, any incumbent body.

## Operating principles

1. **MLS truth vs. public display are separate.** The public portal renders
   only a public-safe projection; private fields are structurally excluded and
   CI-guarded.
2. **Access-only enforcement.** Remedies are contractual and limited to
   platform access — flag, unpublish, suspend, decline — never monetary or
   professional sanction.
3. **Cayman-correct data.** Block & Parcel, district, tenure, and KYD are
   first-class; data accuracy is a membership obligation.
4. **Accountability by design.** Every state change is recorded in an immutable
   audit log.

## Change control

Material change to the no-compensation-lock-in principle requires the same
heightened governance as a Phase 0 reversal and explicit re-approval.
