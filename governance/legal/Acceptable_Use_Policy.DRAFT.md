## 1. Scope

Applies to every user of CIREME (members, advertisers, administrators) and
all content and activity on the platform.

## 2. Listing accuracy (core obligation)

2.1 Listings must be accurate, current, and not misleading. You must
update or withdraw a listing promptly when it is no longer accurate.

2.2 Listings must comply with the **Listing Rulebook**
(`Listing_Rulebook_v1.md`) and the field classification
(`Listing_Field_Classification.md`): required fields complete before a
listing may be public; data placed in the correct public/member-only/
private field.

2.3 No duplicate, stale, fabricated, or bait listings. The platform runs
an automated compliance sweep; detected issues enter the enforcement
ladder.

## 3. Prohibited conduct

You must not:

- Misrepresent a property, price, availability, your role, or your
  authority to list.
- Enter, request, or imply any **commission, buyer-agent compensation, or
  cooperation fee** through the platform. CIREME does not operate these and
  the platform carries no such fields; attempting to repurpose free-text
  fields for this purpose is prohibited.
- Scrape, bulk-extract, resell, or redistribute member-only data or
  contact information, or use it for any purpose other than legitimate
  real-estate activity connected to the platform.
- Misuse the one-way RESO export beyond `[PERMITTED SYNDICATION USES —
  owner/counsel]`; it contains public data only and is member-access
  controlled.
- Circumvent access controls, the membership-approval process, or the
  admin-mediated credential process; share or sell access.
- Upload unlawful, infringing, malicious, or harassing content; harvest
  personal data unlawfully; or breach Cayman Islands law.

## 4. Member-only data and contact routing

4.1 Member-only remarks and routed contact details are provided for
direct, legitimate real-estate communication only. Treat any personal data
you receive in accordance with applicable data-protection law
(`[COUNSEL: member controller/processor duties — cover note Q3]`).

## 5. Enforcement ladder

5.1 Violations are handled progressively and **every action is recorded in
the immutable audit log**:

| Step | Effect |
|---|---|
| Flag | Issue opened; member notified `[NOTICE METHOD]` |
| Request correction | Member must remediate within `[CURE PERIOD]` |
| Unpublish | Listing removed from public/RESO until corrected |
| Remove | Listing removed |
| Account suspension | Access suspended |
| Account termination | Membership ended |

5.2 CIREME may escalate immediately for serious, repeated, or unlawful
conduct. `[APPEAL / REVIEW PROCESS — counsel to define for due process.]`

5.3 False-positive dismissals and all enforcement actions require a
recorded reason and are auditable (single and bulk actions alike).
