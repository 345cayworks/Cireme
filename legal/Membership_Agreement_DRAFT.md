## 1. Parties and scope

This Membership Agreement ("Agreement") is between **[CIREME operating entity
legal name]** ("CIREME", "we", "us") and the person or organization that
registers a member account ("Member", "you").

CIREME operates an online real-estate listing marketplace for the Cayman
Islands. CIREME is a **technology and marketing platform**. CIREME is **not** a
real-estate brokerage, a multiple listing service operated by or affiliated with
the Cayman Islands Real Estate Brokers Association (CIREBA), a self-regulatory
body, an escrow agent, a valuer, or a party to any property transaction.

## 2. Eligibility and member types

You may register as one of:

- **Private Seller (FSBO)** — an owner, or a person duly authorized in writing
  by the owner, listing property in which they hold an interest.
- **Independent Broker / Agent** — a real-estate professional listing on behalf
  of clients. CIREBA membership is **not required and not assumed**. You are
  responsible for holding any registrations, permits, or authorizations required
  to conduct real-estate activity in the Cayman Islands. `[Counsel: confirm
  current Cayman requirements, if any, for real-estate practice.]`
- **Advertiser / Partner** — a service provider listing non-property offerings.

You must be at least 18 and able to form a binding contract. You must provide
accurate registration information and keep it current.

## 3. The no-compensation-lock-in principle (core term)

This is a defining term of the platform, not boilerplate:

1. CIREME does **not** require, set, mediate, publish, or enforce any
   buyer-broker cooperation fee, commission split, or compensation
   arrangement as a condition of listing.
2. CIREME provides **no structured field** for offering or advertising
   cooperation compensation. Any compensation arrangement is solely between the
   relevant parties and outside this platform.
3. A Member must not use free-text fields to circumvent (1)–(2) by creating a
   de-facto mandatory cooperation schedule binding on other Members.
4. Nothing here prevents a seller and their own representative from privately
   agreeing terms off-platform.

## 4. Listing accuracy obligations

You represent and warrant, for each listing you create or control, that:

- You have the right and authority to market the property or offering.
- All facts (price, district, Block & Parcel where applicable, tenure
  — freehold / strata / leasehold, beds/baths, size, status) are accurate and
  not misleading at time of submission.
- Media depicts the actual subject property and you hold the rights to use it
  (see §7).
- You will update status promptly — and in any event within **[X business
  days]** — when the property goes pending, sold, withdrawn, or off-market.
- You will not post duplicate records for the same property.

## 5. Acceptable use

You will not: post false, fraudulent, discriminatory, or unlawful content;
scrape or bulk-extract data except via a CIREME-authorized API; misrepresent
identity or authority; upload malware; harass other users; circumvent access
controls; or use the platform to violate Cayman Islands law, including the
**Data Protection Act, 2017**.

Discrimination note: listings and conduct must comply with applicable Cayman
anti-discrimination law. `[Counsel: specify applicable statute(s) and any

## 6. Enforcement — light-touch and contractual only

CIREME's enforcement authority is **purely contractual** and limited to platform
access. CIREME is **not** a regulator and does **not** adjudicate professional
conduct, licensing, or disputes between Members and their clients.

For accuracy or acceptable-use breaches, CIREME may, proportionately:

1. flag or request correction of a listing;
2. unpublish or remove a listing;
3. suspend or terminate the account;
4. decline future registration.

There is no monetary penalty or professional sanction. We will give notice and
a reasonable chance to cure where practicable, except for clear fraud, legal
risk, or repeated breach. Decisions may be appealed to **[CIREME contact /
process]**; CIREME's determination on platform access is final.

## 7. Content licence and media rights

You retain ownership of content you submit. You grant CIREME a non-exclusive,
worldwide, royalty-free licence to host, display, reproduce, resize, and
distribute that content **for the purpose of operating, marketing, and
syndicating the marketplace** (including the public portal and any authorized
RESO-format export — see §9), for the listing's active life plus a reasonable
archival period of **[retention period]**.

You warrant you hold all rights (including photographer/floorplan rights) in
submitted media. You will indemnify CIREME against third-party claims arising
from content you submitted.
