## 1. Purpose and scope

This Acceptable Use Policy ("AUP") governs all use of the CIREME marketplace by
any account holder or visitor. It operationalizes the conduct obligations in the
Membership Agreement and the locked Phase 0 positioning (Complement + Niche; no
compensation lock-in). Where this AUP and the Membership Agreement conflict, the
Membership Agreement controls.

## 2. Listing integrity

You must:

- list only property or offerings you are authorized to market;
- keep all stated facts (price in KYD, district, Block & Parcel, tenure,
  beds/baths, size, status) accurate and not misleading at submission;
- update listing status promptly when the property goes pending, sold,
  withdrawn, or off-market — within **[X business days]** (must match
  Membership Agreement §4);
- not create duplicate records for the same property (same district + Block &
  Parcel), which the platform flags automatically;
- not use media that misrepresents the subject property.

## 3. The no-compensation-lock-in rule

Consistent with Membership Agreement §3, you must not use any free-text field,
description, media, or document upload to publish, advertise, or impose a
buyer-broker cooperation fee, commission split, or compensation schedule as a
condition affecting other Members. The platform provides no structured field for
this; circumventing that design is a material AUP breach.

## 4. Prohibited conduct

You will not:

- post false, fraudulent, discriminatory, or unlawful content;
- violate applicable Cayman anti-discrimination / fair-marketing law
  `[Counsel: cite applicable statute(s)]`;
- scrape, crawl, or bulk-extract data except via a CIREME-authorized API;
- misrepresent identity, authority, or licensing status;
- upload malware or attempt to probe, scan, or breach platform security;
- circumvent access controls, rate limits, or the public/private data boundary;
- harass, threaten, or impersonate any person;
- use the platform to violate the Data Protection Act, 2017 or any other Cayman
  Islands law.

## 5. Data and security expectations

- Treat enquirer/lead personal data you receive as an independent controller
  with your own lawful basis (see Privacy Policy §6 and Membership Agreement §8).
- Do not export, resell, or repurpose other Members' or enquirers' personal data.
- Report suspected vulnerabilities or data incidents to **[security contact]**
  rather than exploiting or publicizing them.

## 6. Enforcement (access-only, contractual)

Enforcement is purely contractual and limited to platform access, mirroring
Membership Agreement §6. Proportionate measures, with notice and a chance to
cure where practicable: (1) flag / request correction; (2) unpublish or remove a
listing; (3) suspend or terminate the account; (4) decline future registration.
Immediate action may be taken for clear fraud, legal risk, security threat, or
repeated breach. There is no monetary penalty or professional sanction. Appeals:
**[CIREME contact / process]**; CIREME's determination on platform access is
final. Every enforcement action is recorded in the platform's immutable audit
log.

## 7. Changes

CIREME may update this AUP with **[notice period]** notice posted to the
account. Continued use after the effective date constitutes acceptance.
